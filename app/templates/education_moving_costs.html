Money-saving tips

  • Share housing: Split costs like rent, utilities, and internet with housemates.
  • Buy secondhand furniture: Facebook Marketplace, Gumtree, and charity shops have cheap options.
  • Start minimal: You don't need everything on day one. Add furniture as your budget allows.
  • Compare utility providers: Rates shop around for the best deal.vary
  • Plan ahead: Start saving a move-out fund 12 months before you plan to leave home.6
