ABS Data Sources

The numbers and benchmarks used across this website are based on datasets from the Australian Bureau of Statistics, commonly known as the ABS.

The ABS is Australia’s national statistical agency. It collects and publishes official data about population, housing, work, income, cost of living, and other areas that help explain how people live across Australia.

In Hermap, ABS data is used in context to support comparisons around living costs, income, location, and financial readiness. These figures are intended to help users better understand possible trade-offs, not to provide personal financial advice.
