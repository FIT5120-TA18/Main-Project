ABS Data Sources

The numbers and benchmarks used across this website are based on datasets from the Australian Bureau of Statistics, commonly known as the ABS.

The ABS is Australia’s national statistical agency. It collects and publishes official data about population, housing, work, income, cost of living, and other areas that help explain how people live across Australia.

In Hermap, ABS data is used in context to support comparisons around living costs, income, location, and financial readiness. These figures are intended to help users better understand possible trade-offs, not to provide personal financial advice.

Terms of Service

Privacy & Data Collection
This platform does not collect, store, or retain any personal information you provide. All inputs including your age, location, income, and living situation are used solely within your current browser session to generate personalised insights. No data is transmitted to or held on our servers at any point. Your privacy is fully preserved.

Nature of Information & Advice
All insights, comparisons, and recommendations presented on this platform are derived entirely from publicly available data published by the Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS) and other reputable data sources. The information provided is intended for general educational and informational purposes only. It does not constitute financial, legal, or professional advice. Users are encouraged to exercise their own judgement and, where appropriate, seek guidance from a qualified professional before making any financial decisions. This platform serves as a tool to inform, not to prescribe or direct any course of action.
